⚙️ ANIMATION SPECS:
===================

Stat Cards Glow:
- Type: Pulsing Shadow & Border
- Duration: 1.5 seconds
- Easing: EaseInOutCubic
- Alpha Range: 0.3 → 0.8

Feature Cards Shimmer:
- Type: Linear Gradient Shift
- Duration: 2 seconds
- Easing: Linear
- Position: -1 → 1 (full width)

Button Glow:
- Type: Pulsing Shadow & Border
- Duration: 1 second
- Easing: EaseInOutCubic
- Alpha Range: 0.4 → 1.0
