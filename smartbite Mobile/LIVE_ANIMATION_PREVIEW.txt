ACTUAL VISUAL APPEARANCE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

What you'll actually see on screen:

1. STAT CARDS: Gently pulse with a colored glow
   - The glow color matches your selected pathway (Red/Green/Purple)
   - Creates a "breathing" effect - relaxing to watch
   - Border and shadow pulse together
   - Numbers stay bright and readable

2. FEATURE CARDS: Shimmer effect flows across
   - Creates a "liquid" feel across the card
   - Makes the card feel dynamic and alive
   - Borders also pulse during shimmer
   - Very eye-catching and modern

3. BUTTONS: Pulsing glow draws attention
   - Encourages users to tap
   - Faster than stat cards (1s vs 1.5s)
   - More urgent feel
   - Great for CTAs (Call-to-Action)

4. BACKGROUND: Dark navy stays static
   - Provides contrast for glowing elements
   - Prevents visual overload
   - Easy on the eyes
   - Professional appearance

5. OVERALL EFFECT: Premium, modern, animated UI
   - Feels responsive and alive
   - Professional polish
   - Engaging without being distracting
   - Beautiful gradient transitions
